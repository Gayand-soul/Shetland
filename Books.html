Shetland Inspires: The Soul of Shetland in Pictures and Stories

Shetland Inspires is a book that captivates readers with an enchanting blend of images and texts from the unique Scottish archipelago of Shetland. With each page, a world of rugged landscapes, dramatic cliffs, vast moorlands, and charming small villages unfolds, capturing the essence of this remote place.

The book’s photographs are masterfully taken, with every image telling its own story. From the golden hues of sunrise over the sea to the awe-inspiring spectacle of wild storms, the visuals evoke a profound sense of connection to nature’s power and beauty. The accompanying texts complement the images by delving into Shetland’s history, culture, and daily life.

Readers are invited into tales of the proud inhabitants, their traditions, and the unique bond between Shetland and its Nordic heritage. The poetic language enhances the sense of Shetland’s mystique and inspiration. This book is highly recommended for anyone planning a journey to Shetland or longing to dream their way there.

Furthermore, Shetland Inspires makes the perfect gift—for Christmas, weddings, engagements, or birthdays—a present that is guaranteed to inspire and touch the recipient. This book is more than a collection of pages; it’s a journey. A tribute to the soul of Shetland, it leaves readers deeply moved and inspired. The books are being translated into English and will soon be ready. Feel free to order your English copies in advance!

Shetland Between Two Seas: Images and Stories

Shetland Between Two Seas is a captivating book that combines stunning photography with short, poetic texts to showcase the unique charm of Shetland. The book takes readers on a journey through the rugged and dramatic landscapes where the waves of the North Sea and the Atlantic Ocean meet.
The photographs in the book capture Shetland’s breathtaking scenery—cliffs rising from the sea, expansive moorlands, and picturesque villages. Each image conveys a sense of serenity and strength, underscoring the book’s theme of life between two powerful seas.
The accompanying texts provide insight into Shetland’s history, its people, and traditions. With few but carefully chosen words, the reader is invited into stories of a place where nature and culture are inseparable.
This book is highly recommended for travelers, nature enthusiasts, or as a meaningful gift. Whether given as a Christmas present, wedding gift, or birthday surprise, Shetland Between Two Seas is a tribute to a fascinating landscape that inspires and moves the heart.

Lidingö: A New Era – Nature, Culture, and Events

Lidingö: A New Era is a book that captures the essence of Lidingö in a time of change and growth. Through a collection of stunning photographs and concise texts, readers are invited to explore the island’s rich natural beauty, cultural heritage, and vibrant modern events.
The photographs showcase Lidingö’s unique landscapes, from serene forests and coastlines to open meadows and meticulously maintained parks. The beauty of nature is contrasted with the city’s dynamic energy, depicting the meeting of tradition and modernity.
The texts complement the images by highlighting Lidingö’s history, its significance as a cultural hub, and the many events that make the island a lively and thriving place. From art exhibitions and music festivals to sports competitions and local markets, the book offers a glimpse into the diversity of life on Lidingö.
Lidingö: A New Era is an inspiring book for both residents and visitors. It’s a perfect gift for nature enthusiasts, culture lovers, or anyone looking to discover all that Lidingö has to offer.
